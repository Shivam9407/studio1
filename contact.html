FAQ

What can I expect within 48 hours?

You can expect an initial concept or preliminary response within the first 48 hours of onboarding, giving you immediate visibility into our design direction.

How does the subscription model work?

Our subscription model offers unlimited design requests for a flat monthly fee, allowing you to submit projects as needed without additional costs.

Can I pause my subscription if needed?

Yes, you can pause your subscription at any time. Simply notify us before the next billing cycle and your remaining days will be saved for when you return.

Who will I be working with on my projects?

You will be paired directly with our senior designers and motion experts, ensuring high-quality output without any intermediate friction.

How do I get started with Studio?

Book a discovery call with our team. Once we align on your goals, we'll set up your dashboard so you can start submitting requests immediately.

How do revisions work?

Revisions are completely unlimited. We refine the work continuously until you are 100% satisfied with the final result.

Still looking for answers or need a good chat?

Our team will guide you through our design process, project specifications and cost estimate.
