01 / The Brief

Overview

Spotify Wrapped is the world's most anticipated music recap — a moment where 500 million users discover who they are through sound. We were tasked with building the global motion language that transforms raw streaming data into an immersive, share-worthy personal story. Every transition, every color burst, every typographic reveal had to feel earned and cinematic.

02 / Context

The
Challenge

Wrapped runs simultaneously across 180+ markets with data personalized for every single listener. The challenge was architectural: build a motion system flexible enough to scale across unlimited user permutations, while ensuring every individual feels like it was made just for them.

Technically, frame rates on mid-range Android devices had to match the premium feel of flagship iOS — every animation had to be GPU-composited and pixel-perfect across 500 million unique instances.

03 / Execution

The Solution

We created a universal motion grammar — a living system of transitions, data morphs, and typographic explosions that scales from a single listener to the entire planet, all driven by real musical energy.

01

Data Choreography

Every bar chart, every percentage reveal is a choreographed motion event — not a static graphic. Numbers fly in, bars grow on the beat, playlists materialize from particle clouds.

02

Adaptive Color Engine

We extracted dominant colors from each user's top artist album artwork and remapped the entire UI palette in real time — making every Wrapped feel like a unique art piece.

03

60fps Everywhere

Using compositor-layer animations exclusively — transform, opacity, and filter — we guaranteed 60fps performance on devices as old as 3 years without a single JavaScript jank.
